Theme

What is the foundation of one idea that is always worth spreading? The answer is harmony. We believe that so many problems in this and other societies could be solved if we could all come together and sing the same tune. Where do we find this tune? In the most obvious place of all: music. Music is a universal language that has the power to unify people and break down barriers between them regardless of race, gender, nationality, language, or creed. This TEDx talk will be inspired by music and the concepts and ideas that music provides.
