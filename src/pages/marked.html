Theme

There have always been those who, throughout time, have stood out, stark against the gray backdrop of mediocrity. It is those people people who have been the innovators, the world changers, the unique. They are the people that cannot help but stand out, recognized as different from the others around them. It is in their eyes there is reflected the drumbeats of a dance that not all can hear for they are "Marked."They paint the world in dynamic colors, making their vision of the world clearly evident and beautiful, for all to see. This is the theme that calls to them, "Marked" with confidence, "Marked" with wonder, "Marked" with determination. This is the theme is for all the people who long to scream at the world:

This is me!
This is who I AM!
This is how I leave #MyMark!
